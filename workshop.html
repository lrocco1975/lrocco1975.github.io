IV Workshop on Social Capital and Health

Padova, Italy — October 4–5, 2012

Overview

The Fourth Global Network on Social Capital and Health Workshop was held at Palazzo Bo, University of Padova, on October 4–5, 2012. The best papers presented at the workshop were published in a symposium issue of the Eastern Economic Journal.

Scientific Committee

The workshop featured an international scientific committee of 11 prominent economists from institutions in Europe and North America working on social capital and health.

The Global Network on Social Capital and Health

This workshop was organised under the auspices of the Global Network on Social Capital and Health, an international research network connecting economists and health researchers across five continents.
